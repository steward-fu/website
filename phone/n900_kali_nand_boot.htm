手機 - Nokia N900 - Kali - 如何從Flash開機


1. Partition 1格式化成FAT32
2. Partition 2格式化成Ext3
3. Partition 3格式化成Swap
4. Kali的rootfs複製到Partition 2
5. Bootmenu 

$ sudo vim /etc/bootmenu.d/30-kali.item 
    ITEM_NAME="Kali Linux kali-rolling armhf 4.5.0-rc1+"
    ITEM_DEVICE="${INT_CARD}p1"
    ITEM_FSTYPE="fat"
    ITEM_KERNEL="/uImage-4.5.0-rc1+"
    ITEM_INITRD="/uInitrd-4.5.0-rc1+"
    ITEM_CMDLINE="root=/dev/mmcblk1p2 rootwait ro console=tty0 vram=12M ubi.mtd=5"

$ u-boot-update-bootmenu
