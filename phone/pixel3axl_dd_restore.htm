手機 - Google Pixel 3a XL - Droidian - 如何更新回原本boot.img


步驟如下： 

$ cd
$ wget https://github.com/droidian-images/rootfs-api28gsi-all/releases/download/droidian%2Fbullseye%2F23/droidian-adaptation-google-sargo-arm64_20211127.zip
$ unzip droidian-adaptation-google-sargo-arm64_20211127.zip
$ fastboot flash boot droidian-adaptation-google-sargo-arm64_20211127/boot.img
