問題如下： 

$ fastboot format:ext4 userdata
    Warning: userdata type is f2fs, but ext4 was requested for formatting.
    mke2fs 1.44.5 (15-Dec-2018)

    Warning: RAID stripe-width 1 not an even multiple of stride 2.

    /tmp/TemporaryFile-hADjY5: Unimplemented ext2 library function while setting up superblock
    /usr/lib/android-sdk/platform-tools/mke2fs failed with status 1
    mke2fs failed: 1

解法如下： 

使用新版platform-tools，如：platform-tools_r33.0.3-linux.zip
