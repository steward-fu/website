手機 - N900 - MAEMO LESTE - 安裝系統


參考資訊：
https://maedevu.maemo.org/images/
https://leste.maemo.org/Nokia_N900

PC 

$ cd
$ wget https://maedevu.maemo.org/images/n900/20231224-chimaera/maemo-leste-1.0-armhf-n900-20231224.img.xz
$ xz -d maemo-leste-1.0-armhf-n900-20231224.img.xz
$ sudo dd if=maemo-leste-1.0-armhf-n900-20231224.img of=/dev/sdX bs=1M

Maemo 

$ sudo vim /etc/bootmenu.d/30-maemo-leste-Overclock.item
    ITEM_NAME="Maemo Leste Overclock"
    ITEM_KERNEL="uImageX"
    ITEM_DEVICE="${EXT_CARD}p1"
    ITEM_FSTYPE="ext2"

$ u-boot-update-bootmenu
