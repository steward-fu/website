build buildroot

編譯步驟如下： 

$ cd
$ wget https://buildroot.org/downloads/buildroot-2019.02.4.tar.gz
$ tar xvf buildroot-2019.02.4.tar.gz
$ cd buildroot-2019.02.4/
$ wget https://github.com/steward-fu/pocketbeagle/releases/download/v1.0/config_buildroot-2019.02.4
$ mv config_buildroot-2019.02.4 .config
$ make -j4
