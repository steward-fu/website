系統 - Debian - LD_LIBRARY_PATH、--library-path


參考資訊：
https://stackoverflow.com/questions/13428910/how-to-set-the-environment-variable-ld-library-path-in-linux

LD_LIBRARY_PATH 

$ export LD_LIBRARY_PATH=my_lib_path:$LD_LIBRARY_PATH
$ my_elf_file

--library-path 

$ /lib/ld-linux.so.2 --library-path my_lib_path my_elf_file

P.S. ld-linux.so.2會依照EFL檔案而異，如：ld-linux-x86-64.so.2
