系統 - Sailfish OS - 安裝環境


參考資訊：
https://github.com/sailfishos
https://docs.sailfishos.org/Tools/Sailfish_SDK/Installation/

步驟如下： 

$ cd
$ wget https://releases.sailfishos.org/sdk/installers/3.9.6/SailfishSDK-3.9.6-linux64-offline.run
$ chmod a+x SailfishSDK-3.9.6-linux64-offline.run
$ ./SailfishSDK-3.9.6-linux64-offline.run
